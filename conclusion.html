Centralized Food Ordering Portal: Virginia Tech

Conclusion

Centralized Food Ordering System is a system dedicated to VT Dining halls and Vendors interested to sell their products to VT Dining Services. This system automates the ordering process thereby reducing the manual workload of Managers and Vendors. The system has 3 dedicated modules i.e. Southgate Module, Vendor Module and Dining Hall module. Each of these modules cater to different intended users.
